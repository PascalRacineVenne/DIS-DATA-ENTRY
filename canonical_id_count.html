TCFD is the narrowest framework — climate only, 4 pillars. Almost all its data points are already covered by CSRD E1 and IFRS S2. Net new IDs from TCFD alone: near zero.
TCFD was dissolved in 2023 and absorbed into IFRS S2. In practice, if you cover CSRD E1 + IFRS S2, TCFD is covered automatically. No dedicated TCFD-only canonical IDs are needed.
