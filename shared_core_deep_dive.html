These ~120 canonical IDs are shared across at least two frameworks — most appear in all five. They represent the minimum viable data collection for a multi-framework ESG SaaS. A user who fills these in will automatically have 75–85% of any single framework's quantitative requirements satisfied.

Framework coverage dots: filled = mandatory or standard · faded = conditional / if material. Note that TCFD is omitted as a separate column — its data points are fully covered by CSRD E1 and IFRS S2, adding zero net-new canonical IDs.
